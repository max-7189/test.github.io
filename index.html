Welcome to Fruit Tracker

This system uses blockchain technology to track the supply chain of fruits from the farm to the retailer.



How to use

To track the status of a fruit, go to the Track Fruit page and enter the fruit ID.


To add a new item, go to the Add Fruit page


To update the status of a fruit, go to the Update Status page and enter the required information.


Contact
